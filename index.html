About Us

Located at 2240 US Highway 130, North Brunswick, NJ 08902, Thai Lotus Thai Cuisine offers a cozy atmosphere where you can enjoy traditional Thai dishes made with love and the freshest ingredients. Our menu features a variety of options, including vegetarian and halal choices, ensuring there's something for everyone.

Our customers love dishes like the Massaman Curry, Pad Thai, and our unique Mango Chicken. Whether you're dining in or ordering takeout, we strive to provide an exceptional culinary experience.
